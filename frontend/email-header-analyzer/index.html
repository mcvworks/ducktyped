Email Header Analyzer

Parse and analyze email headers to troubleshoot delivery issues. Check SPF, DKIM, DMARC authentication and trace email routing path.

How to get email headers:
Gmail: Open email → Click ⋮ (three dots) → "Show original" → Copy all text
Outlook: Open email → File → Properties → Copy "Internet headers"
Yahoo: Open email → More → "View Raw Message"
Apple Mail: View → Message → All Headers → Select and copy

What we analyze:
• Email routing path (all servers the email passed through)
• Delivery timeline and delays
• Sender information (IP, domain, SPF/DKIM/DMARC status)
• Authentication results (SPF, DKIM, DMARC)
• Spam score and filtering information
• Reply-To and Return-Path details
• Message-ID and threading information
